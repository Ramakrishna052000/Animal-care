About us

We are a NGO who mainly aim in reaching out all the animals who doesn't require a special care. Our animal shelter was established in January 2013 with the aim of providing timely medical aid to injured and ill animals of the street in Shivamogga. We strive to provide dignity to animals, who are old, blind, paraplegic & suffering from chronic diseases and allow them to live out their lives at the shelter in a safe & peaceful environment. We provide home to many dogs,cats,rabbits, ducks,hens,pigs,guinea pigs,birds and terrapins. Apart from rescue & shelter services, we also provide adoptions, humane education in schools, animal cruelty prevention & control and volunteering opportunities.

Mission

We aim to provide life saving veterinary care on par with private veterinary setups to all animals, irrespective of their ownership status. We have a rescue helpline, animal ambulance services and a fully equipped veterinary trauma care unit. No animal left behind is our main motto.
